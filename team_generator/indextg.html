Team Generator

Take the stress out of choosing your game day team...

Welcome to Team Generator!

This is Team Generator, a decision making application that will allow you to auto generate the best possible team for any given game. Team Generator selects players from panels that you have previously created based on the requirements that are chosen by you for each specific game.

How does Team Generator work?

  • Create your Players

    Player profiles must be created for all the available players that will feature in your panel. Add personal information as well as player statistics for each player created.

  • Create your Panel

    Panels must be created from the selection of players that you have previously created. These panels are then used in order to select a team for any given game

  • Create your Team

    Allow Team Generator to select the optimal team for your upcoming match. As a coach or manager you may set out the individual player requirements for what is needed in any given position for any given game. Once these reqirements have been entered into the Team Generator system each position on the team is filled with the optimal player based on the outlined requirements.
